# Japan Cherry Blossom Historical Data

Historical series of phenological data for cherry tree flowering at Kyoto City.

## Files

- `cherry_blossoms.csv` — annual records of first bloom date and March temperature estimates

## Variables

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| `year` | Year CE |
| `doy` | Day of year of first bloom (day 89 = April 1, day 119 = May 1) |
| `temp` | March temperature estimate |
| `temp_upper` | Upper 95% bound for temperature estimate |
| `temp_lower` | Lower 95% bound for temperature estimate |
